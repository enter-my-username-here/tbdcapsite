Our Mission

At TBD Capital, our mission is to identify and acquire a small business with loyal customers and a strong history of recurring revenue, with the goal of building upon its established success. We are committed to preserving the legacy of the businesses we acquire by maintaining the highest standards of quality and excellence in all areas of the business. Our approach combines strategic vision with a fresh perspective to drive sustainable growth and deliver value to customers and investors alike. Whether you are an entrepreneur seeking to transition your business or an investor interested in promising opportunities, we invite you to explore how TBD Capital can partner with you to achieve continued success.

Our Team

At TBD Capital, small business entrepreneurship is in our DNA. Our team of passionate and driven individuals is deeply rooted in the world of small business, with both founders coming from families that have successfully owned and operated small businesses for over 20 years. This heritage has instilled in us a profound understanding of the challenges and rewards of building and growing businesses. Combining our experience in both public and private markets with a genuine entrepreneurial spirit, we are uniquely equipped to navigate the complexities of the business landscape and achieve extraordinary results.
